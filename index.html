Circle
 Brand development
 Squarespace
 2017
Squarespace Circle caters to the community of creatives, developers, and designers who use Squarespace to build websites for themselves and their clients. In order to accommodate the diverse, growing community, Circle recognizes certain members with unique badges. Previously, each badge was designed independent of one another. The new badges are based off a single system. Each contain a unique icon based off a unified grid and are paired with a fixed type lockup.
