Rio de Janerio
Description

An identity system for the World Heritage Site Rio de Janeiro: Carioca Landscapes between the Mountain and the Sea.


I began by developing a mark to represent the site. I chose the typefaces and color palette that felt true to Rio.


The mark was then used on an envelope, letter- head, & advertisements.


Information about the site and the World Heritage Centre was brought together in a booklet. Featuring highlighs of Rio and other site around the world.
