Nike & El Lissitzky:
This booklet relates El Lissitzky to the contemporary design of Nike Sneakers. Both Lissitzky and Nike have pushed their fields into a new realm. With his Proun series, Lissitzky worked to create a new art for the post-revolutionary world through the use of space and architectural form. Nike continues to questioned what exactly is a sneaker. Under their reign, sneakers have gone from something only professional athletes wear, to something commonly worn by all, to an art object, to collectible item. Their Air Jordan line has caught the attention of millions of people across the globe creating a underground market of buying and selling. →
