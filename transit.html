Description

Most sites online are essientally a collection. For example, Amazon
is a collection of items
for sale.


I choose to represent the collection of bikes and bike parts that have collected in my apartment throughout the year.


These items cluttered our already small space and I wanted to express that with how I presented these items on a website.
