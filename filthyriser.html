Filthy Riser
Description

I thought it would be fun to have an on campus obsticle course race so I made it happen.


I design the course, convinced RISD to fund it, marketed it to fellow students, and raced.


This was a semester long undertaking and featured two mud pits, a cargo net climb, string maze, and bad prom photo finish.


I colaborated with almost every department in RISD to make this happen and look forward to watching it continue to grow in the years to come.
