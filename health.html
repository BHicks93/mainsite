This 36 page publication investigates various subjects, writing, and products currently surrounding health as well as proposes some of its own. It is a mix of fact and fiction, real and fake, and much like the world it leaves you to decide for yourself what to believe. The goal is to pique your interest, highlight the importance of health, and begin a conversation.
