Senior Open Studio
Description

As graphic design department representative I was in charge of organizing Senior Open Studios.


I began by recruiting a team of seniors to brainstorm and come up with an overall theme.


"Sweet night with GD."


We thought of all things sweet and pink. We sent out pink sprinkles in dime bags as promotion, coated the posters in sugar, and made the entire room turn pink.
